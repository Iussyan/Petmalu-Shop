🐾 Petmalu Shop: How it All Works (Designer's Guide)

Building this game is all about creating a cozy cycle of love and care. Here is how we make the magic happen:

1. The Grand Tour (Exploration)
The player starts their journey in the Town, but as they grow their shop's reputation, they unlock new, beautiful places like the Forest, Beach, and Mountains. Each area has its own unique "vibe" and specific animals waiting to be found.

2. A New Friend (Rescue)
While wandering, the player will encounter animals that need help. Whether they are lost or injured, the player "collects" them from the wild. The game is smart—it remembers exactly which animal was found and what condition it was in when it was rescued.

3. Coming Home (The Shop)
When the player steps into the Pet Shop, all the animals they rescued are waiting for them. We don't just "move" the animals; we carefully recreate them in the shop based on the memory of the rescue. This ensures they "remember" their stats and health from the wild.

4. Tender Loving Care (Management)
Inside the shop, the real work begins. Every animal has three big needs:
- Tummy Time (Hunger): They need regular feeding.
- Doctor Visits (Health): They need medicine and rest.
- Play Time (Happiness): They need to be happy!
We track these numbers to make each pet feel like a living, breathing creature.

5. Happy Tail-Wags (Adoption)
Once a pet is healthy, fed, and happy, they are ready for adoption. This is the ultimate goal! Rehoming a pet gives the player coins and boosts the shop's reputation, which is the key to exploring those new, locked areas.

6. The Seamless Feel (Transitions)
To keep the player immersed in the "cozy" world, we use smooth screen fades. When walking through a door, the world gently goes black and then fades back in. It’s like a scene change in a movie, making the transition between the wild and the shop feel effortless.
